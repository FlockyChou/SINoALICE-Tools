About Experience Calculator
Description

Basically, this tools takes in EXP needed to level up and your AP remaining and calculates an EXP/AP ratio. Then it looks through all the Story chapters to see if there is a chapter with a similar (but less than) ratio. It then repeats this process based on the ratio until it can no longer do that. Finally, it recommends the largest chapter you can possibly do to level up.

Long story short, it's really bad and you should not use this tool.
